Quality & Compliance

As Importer of Record, PETS verifies suppliers, documents hazards, and maintains FSVP/HACCP-aligned controls for Egyptian herbs & botanicals delivered into the U.S. Every shipment is backed by COA, traceability, and retained records.

Programs & Standards

  • FSVP (FDA): Supplier approval, hazard analysis, verification activities, and recordkeeping.
  • HACCP-aligned: Controlled processing at origin (cleaning, sieving, metal detection where applicable).
  • COA & Lab Testing: Microbiology & pesticide panels, moisture, ash, adulteration checks.
  • Traceability: Lot/batch IDs from field to port; chain-of-custody retained.
  • Allergen & Label: Statements available; U.S. labeling guidance on request.

Documentation You’ll Receive

  • COA per lot (micro + pesticide, where relevant)
  • Product Spec Sheet (cut/sieve, moisture targets)
  • Commercial docs (Invoice, PL, BL) + HS codes
  • Traceability map (lot/batch references)

Supplier Requirements

Our Egyptian growers & processors are approved and periodically re-verified.

Approval

Facility profile, hazard analysis, prior compliance history, and capacity review.

Controls

Cleaning, sieving, FM removal, metal detection (where installed), pest control, sanitation.

Verification

COA and third-party panels; shipment-level document checks; corrective actions as needed.
